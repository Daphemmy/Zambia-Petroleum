Zambis Petroleum Limited

Our Identity

Zambis Petroleum Limited is a dynamic and forward-thinking petroleum company based in Zambia, dedicated to becoming a cornerstone of the nation's energy sector. Established with a vision to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy solutions, we are committed to delivering high-quality petroleum products and services that fuel Zambia's economic growth and development.

Zambis Petroleum Limited emerges as a renewed iteration in the evolving energy landscape. A rebranded and revitalized successor to local petroleum entities shaped by visionary leadership. At its core is Benedict Adeniran, the seasoned entrepreneur and oil mogul who founded Aiteo in 2008 and later led it to prominence across upstream, downstream, and power sectors thisdaylive.co With deep roots in Ocean and Oil (now Oando) in the mid-1990s and an upward trajectory through roles in MRS Oil and banking Peters launched Sigmund Communecci in 1999—Aiteo’s precursor—which evolved into a fully integrated energy powerhouse by 2008 . Now, in 2025, the reformation of this legacy into Zambis Petroleum Limited aims to consolidate lessons learned and forge a new path within both domestic and international markets.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative and sustainable petroleum solutions in Zambis, contributing to the nation's energy security and economic prosperity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to supply high-quality petroleum products and services that meet the evolving needs of our customers while adhering to the highest standards of safety, environmental stewardship, and corporate responsibility.

Our Services

Zambis Petroleum Limited offers a comprehensive range of services across the petroleum value chain:

  • Importation and Distribution: We import refined petroleum products and distribute them through a network of strategically located depots and service stations across Zambis.
  • Storage and Logistics: Our state-of-the-art storage facilities ensure the safe and efficient storage of petroleum products, while our logistics network guarantees timely delivery to our customers.
  • Retail Services: We operate a growing network of service stations that provide fuel and lubricants to consumers, along with convenience store offerings.
  • Wholesale Supply: We supply bulk petroleum products to commercial and industrial clients, including mining companies, manufacturing plants, and agricultural enterprises.

Infrastructure and Operations

Our operations are supported by a robust infrastructure that includes:

  • Storage Facilities: We have invested in modern storage facilities equipped with advanced safety systems to ensure the secure storage of petroleum products.
  • Transportation Network: Our fleet of tankers and distribution vehicles enables efficient transportation of products to various locations across the country.
  • Service Stations: Our expanding network of service stations provides convenient access to fuel and related services for consumers nationwide.

Commitment to Safety and Environment

At Zambis Petroleum Limited, safety and environmental protection are paramount. We adhere to stringent safety protocols to protect our employees, customers, and the communities in which we operate. Our environmental management practices are designed to minimize our impact on the environment, and we continuously seek ways to improve our sustainability performance.

Community Engagement

We recognize the importance of contributing to the communities we serve. Through various corporate social responsibility initiatives, we support education, healthcare, and infrastructure development projects that enhance the well-being of Zambians.

Looking Ahead

As Zambis energy needs continue to grow, Zambis Petroleum Limited is poised to play a pivotal role in meeting these demands. We are committed to expanding our operations, investing in renewable energy sources, and exploring innovative solutions that will ensure a sustainable and prosperous energy future for Zambis.
